Ethereum Staking after Pectra

New validator lifecycle from deposit transaction to active, with the two waiting stages that matter: pending_deposits and the implicit activation queue.
pipeline/state transition explicit queue activation flow

Operational reading

  • No extra queue exists before pending_deposits. Once the deposit transaction is included in a valid canonical execution block and processed by the consensus client, it is appended to state.pending_deposits.
  • pending_deposits is the only explicit pre-activation queue. A deposit can wait there because of finalization constraints, the per-epoch processing cap, or churn budget.
  • After the deposit is applied, balance can already be visible while activation_eligibility_epoch is still unset. That is due to epoch-processing order, not a third hidden queue.
  • The activation queue is implicit. It starts once activation_eligibility_epoch is set, and then waits for finalization and activation/exit churn before activation_epoch is assigned.

Timing intuition

  • Epoch = 32 slots.
  • Slot = 12 seconds.
  • One epoch ≈ 6.4 minutes.
  • After a deposit is applied from pending_deposits, expect up to roughly one more epoch before activation_eligibility_epoch gets set.

Clean wording for your page

  • Stage 1: EL inclusion. Not a queue.
  • Stage 2: pending_deposits. Explicit consensus queue.
  • Stage 3: activation queue. Implicit queue based on validator fields and finalization/churn.
